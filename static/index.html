How to find your authKey
  1. Log in to web.strem.io (Chrome preferred).
  2. Open developer tools (F12 or right-click → Inspect) and switch to the Console tab.
  3. Paste this snippet and press Enter: 
    JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem("profile")).auth.key
  4. Copy the printed key (without quotation marks) and paste it into the field above.

How we protect your credentials

  • Your username/password or auth key is sent over HTTPS directly to Watchly.
  • Credentials are stored only inside Redis behind a salted, deterministic token; the token is all that ever appears in manifest URLs.
  • Tokens never expire by default; rotate your Stremio password or auth key if you ever need a new token.

Need to rotate your access? Re-run this configurator with updated credentials or auth key to instantly refresh your private token.
